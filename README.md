# China Travel 🇨🇳

An interactive map for foreign travelers to discover what makes each place in China special — food, history, landscapes, and culture.

## What it is
Click or hover on any city to explore its landmarks, local dishes, and hidden gems. Each place has its own story told in its own way.

## Status
Work in progress. Building in public.

## Built with
HTML, JavaScript, Leaflet.js
